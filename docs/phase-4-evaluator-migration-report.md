# Phase 4: Teacher Review Evaluator Migration Report

## Refactoring Overview
The Evaluator Agent has been formally refactored into a comprehensive **Teacher Review Agent** to validate output quality, safety guidelines, and student progress metrics.

This refactoring includes:
*   **Academic Validation**: Ensures factual accuracy and alignment with curriculum guidelines.
*   **Exam Alignment Validation**: Verifies exam readiness and coverage of syllabus requirements for KCET and NEET.
*   **Response Quality Validation**: Verifies explanation formatting, tone, length, and readability.
*   **Safety Validation**: Scans for out-of-scope content, prompt injection attempts, and curriculum boundary violations (added `safety_score` to the `TeacherReviewModel`).
*   **Student Assessment**: Provides detailed grading (score, max_score, feedback, missing_points, revision_tip) for MCQs and subjective inputs.

---

## Routing Validation Rules
*   **Mandatory Execution**:
    *   `quiz_generation`: Every quiz generated by the `QuizAgent` is routed through the Teacher Review validation layer.
    *   `answer_evaluation`: Every student response submission is routed to determine correctness.
*   **Conditional Execution**:
    *   `KCET` & `NEET` exam prep queries.
    *   `lesson_summary` & `quick_notes` generator runs.
    *   Low confidence SME responses (< 0.85 threshold).
